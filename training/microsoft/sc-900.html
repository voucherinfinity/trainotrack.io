About SC 900


The Microsoft SC-900 certification, also known as the Microsoft Security, Compliance, and Identity Fundamentals certification, is intended for anybody seeking a foundational grasp of security, compliance, and identity concepts inside the Microsoft ecosystem. This entry-level certification is excellent for beginners and non-technical stakeholders who need to understand fundamental security and compliance principles.


The SC-900 exam covers important topics like understanding security approaches, designing security solutions, safeguarding identities and access, preserving data, and managing governance and compliance concerns in Microsoft 365 and Azure systems. Candidates will learn about Microsoft's security and compliance offerings, such as Microsoft Defender, Azure Active Directory, and Azure Security Centre.

Obtaining the Microsoft SC-900 certification demonstrates that individuals have a fundamental understanding of security, compliance, and identity concepts as they apply to Microsoft products and services. It demonstrates their ability to adequately express these concepts to stakeholders and collaborate with technical teams to develop appropriate solutions.

This certification is useful for a variety of individuals, including sales and marketing professionals, project managers, and business analysts, who need to understand Microsoft's security and compliance capabilities to better serve their organizations and clients. It also serves as a strong foundation for anyone interested in obtaining additional certifications in Microsoft's security and compliance domains.

Preparing for the SC-900 exam includes reviewing Microsoft's security and compliance resources, conducting hands-on labs, and understanding how to apply security and compliance principles in real-world circumstances. Obtaining the SC-900 certification enhances an individual's reputation and ability to effectively contribute to the company's security and compliance activities using Microsoft technologies.

About ISO/IEC 27701


The ISO/IEC 27701 Cybersecurity IT Certification is a well-known accreditation for cybersecurity professionals seeking to learn about privacy and information management. This certification is based on the ISO/IEC 27701 standard, which builds upon the ISO/IEC 27001 and ISO/IEC 27002 standards by stressing privacy management. It provides a comprehensive framework for managing personally identifiable information (PII) while meeting worldwide data protection standards.


A mastery of Privacy Information Management Systems (PIMS) is a prerequisite for attaining the ISO/IEC 27701 certification. Those seeking this certification should be able to show competency in creating, putting into place, and overseeing PIMS in an organizational setup. The details that the certification delves into include but are not limited to topics on privacy risk assessment and data protection impact assessments as well as how these efforts dovetail with the implementation of privacy controls. It focuses on marrying these layers of privacy action to any preexisting ISMS efforts, ensuring that the approach taken towards data protection and cybersecurity is all-encompassing. This is what a successful ISO/IEC 27701 certification hinges on striking a balance between various aspects while maintaining a high level of professionalism in both PIMS design and management within an organization.

The certificate is especially advantageous to people within the IT field— as well as data protection officers and compliance managers— who play a role in the protection of PII and ensuring that regulatory laws are followed. Through this certification, individuals can demonstrate their competency in privacy management which means they bring added value to their companies; they will have the capacity to address intricate challenges related to privacy and be able to mitigate risks.

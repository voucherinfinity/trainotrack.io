About Solution Architect Professional


The AWS Certified Solutions Architect – Professional certification is an elite credential for experienced IT professionals who design and deploy highly available and fault-tolerant systems on Amazon Web Services (AWS). This certification validates advanced technical expertise in the design and deployment of dynamic, scalable, highly available, fault-tolerant, and reliable applications on AWS. Candidates for the Solutions Architect – Professional certification must demonstrate their knowledge of Amazon’s network of remote servers that comprise the AWS Cloud (EC2, S3, VPC, IAM, RDS, and other services) and their ability to design and deploy scalable, highly available, fault-tolerant, and reliable applications on the AWS platform. They must know how to migrate large, multi-tiered applications to AWS; implement cost control strategies; and ensure customer data integrity.


The exam, designed to be one of the landscape’s more challenging offerings, poses a series of in-depth scenarios involving detailed descriptions of an organization’s business objectives and IT requirements, which a candidate must translate into architectural recommendations regarding applications that have recently been implemented, deployed and provisioned on AWS. Candidates are challenged to evaluate requirements and develop architectural recommendations for the implementation, deployment, and provisioning of applications that are migrating to AWS.

The AWS Certified Solutions Architect – Professional is designed for solutions architects who already have two or more years of hands-on experience designing and deploying cloud-based applications with AWS. This exam demonstrates expertise in designing and deploying complex workloads that use not only multiple AWS services but also various architectural design principles.
This certification is a testament to your commitment to AWS professional development and to career advancement. It will open many doors, especially those holding promise for more money and challenging pursuits.

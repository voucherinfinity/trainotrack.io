About Advanced Network Specialty


The AWS Certified Advanced Networking - Specialty certification is designed for professionals who do complicated networking activities and develop networking solutions on the AWS platform. This certification confirms your ability to design, develop, and deploy scalable solutions for enterprise-wide networks on AWS. It is perfect for network practitioners and architects who want to demonstrate their knowledge in managing and securing network architecture on AWS.


This certification covers a wide range of advanced networking concepts, such as designing and deploying large-scale AWS and hybrid IT network infrastructures, as well as automating AWS networking activities. Candidates should be familiar with tools and services such as AWS Direct Connect, AWS Transit Gateway, and Amazon Virtual Private Cloud (VPC). They must also understand and apply AWS networking best practices including network security, compliance, and governance, as well as how to integrate AWS networking services with third-party solutions.

To earn this certification, students must pass the AWS Certified Advanced Networking - Specialty exam, which consists of multiple-choice and multiple-response questions. The exam assesses the candidate's knowledge of five areas: network design, implementation, and deployment; network security; network administration and automation; and monitoring and troubleshooting. The certification process necessitates a thorough understanding of the AWS Well-Architected Framework, including high availability and fault tolerance in network design.

To earn this certification, candidates must demonstrate a thorough understanding of various AWS database services, such as relational, non-relational, and data warehousing databases. They must understand how to choose the best database service for their needs, create highly available and fault-tolerant database systems, and optimize AWS database performance.

This certification requires both practical experience with AWS networking and a thorough understanding of broad networking concepts. AWS offers a variety of materials to assist candidates prepare, such as whitepapers, practice examinations, and training courses. Earning the AWS Certified Advanced Networking - Specialty certification validates professionals' knowledge of managing and upgrading AWS network solutions, extending their career opportunities, and demonstrating their advanced networking skills in the AWS ecosystem.

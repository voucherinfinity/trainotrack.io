What is the Exam pattern of the GRE?

GRE is divided into three parts: writing, verbal reasoning, and quantitative reasoning. The analytical writing element is always first, while the other components, such as verbal reasoning, numerical reasoning, and unscored, can be done in any sequence. Furthermore, trends may differ across online and paper-based assessments.
